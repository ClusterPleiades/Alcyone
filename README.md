## About
<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81938036/150637067-70c696aa-2fce-4621-a08b-9d2545bda54f.png" width="256" height="507"> <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81938036/150637069-08bfafe7-f105-461c-9b28-02032a1ee79a.png" width="256" height="507">

Alcyone is an android application for scheduling by personal assistant character.  
Proceed with the story by registering and completing the schedule.  
Costumes are unlocked as the story progresses or a specific situation occurs.
<br><br>

## Features
<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81938036/150637415-7d4a7680-c833-4827-b572-950681d09dde.png" width="256" height="333">

Switch screens by selecting feature in the navigation drawer.  
We can also click the interaction button to return to alcyone anywhere.
<br><br>

### Alcyone
<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81938036/150637150-d2eda167-210c-462a-b3d8-fdbf5189c90b.png" width="256" height="507">

Talk to alcyone by clicking floating button.  
This provides the overall service of the application,  
such as closing the schedule and proceeding with the story.
<br><br>

### Schedule
<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81938036/150643071-180d635f-e944-416a-9d7a-4fed6a1d6915.png" width="256" height="507">

Add, edit and complete your schedules.  
The reaction of Alcyone might be changed depending on the number of completed schedules.
<br><br>

### Story
<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81938036/150643316-9a9aec72-eab7-437e-921c-6d35a3b328f4.png" width="256" height="507">

Select a previous story to watch again.
<br><br>

### Costume
<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81938036/150643470-5cb02377-ebf7-40b0-98b0-512147fdae5d.png" width="256" height="507">

<br><br>

### Settings
<img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/81938036/150643743-1e963ee6-163c-4caf-b2a2-d2a2a1bfed2d.png" width="256" height="196">

- Set Launch Fragment as Schedule  
Display schedule screen rather than Alcyone when launch application.

- Increase Speed of Conversation  
Increase the speed of reading text when talking.

- Low Specification Mode  
Remove conversation animations.

- Rename  
Change the name Alcyone will call you.
